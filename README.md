# 📉 ChurnGuard

ChurnGuard is an AI-powered customer retention intelligence dashboard that predicts churn risk, explains why customers may leave, and helps product and customer success teams prioritize high-impact retention actions.

🔗 **Live Demo (coming soon):** [Add your Streamlit link here]

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- 🔮 Predicts customer churn using machine learning  
- ⚠️ Classifies customers into risk levels (Low, Medium, High)  
- 💡 Explains churn drivers in plain language  
- 💰 Calculates revenue at risk  
- 📊 Interactive dashboard for decision-making  
- 🎯 Prioritizes high-value customers for retention  

---

## 🧠 Product Thinking

ChurnGuard is designed not just as a model, but as a **decision-support system** that helps teams:

- Identify which customers to act on first  
- Understand why customers are at risk  
- Connect product usage to business outcomes  

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

Python • Streamlit • Scikit-learn • Pandas • Plotly  

---

## 💥 Why This Matters

Customer churn directly impacts revenue. ChurnGuard enables teams to move from reactive support to proactive retention by combining data, AI, and product thinking into one system.
